# MeetMux User Segmentation and Marketing Strategy

## RFM Framework
- **Recency (R)**: Days since last Mux event.
- **Frequency (F)**: Total events attended.
- **Monetary (M)**: Total credits/money spent.

## Marketing Strategy for Push Notifications

### Champions (555)
**Profile:** Users who attended an event recently, attend frequently, and spend the most.
**Strategy:**
- Focus on exclusivity and loyalty rewards rather than discounts.
- Invite them to premium or early-access MeetMux events.
- Send personalized push notifications acknowledging their loyalty ("Thanks for being a top member! Here is an exclusive invite for you").
- Use them for referral campaigns, as they are highly engaged and likely to advocate for the platform.

### At-Risk (111)
**Profile:** Users who haven't attended an event in a long time, rarely attend, and spend very little.
**Strategy:**
- Focus on re-engagement and overcoming barriers to entry.
- Send compelling win-back offers with significant discounts or free credits to incentivize their return.
- Highlight new features, trending events, or content they might have missed ("We miss you! Here's a 50% discount on your next event").
- Ensure push notifications are sent at optimal times to catch their attention without being overly aggressive to avoid app uninstalls.
